Hackergarten Lausanne

Contributing to OSS one line at a time

Quick intro

We are a group of passionate software engineers that tries to meet regularly to contribute together to Open Source Software.

Goals

Everytime we meet, we try to get out of the meetup with a visible contribution that we present to each other and that we advertise here. We want to

  • Contribute
  • Learn
  • Share

Rules

Actually, those are more guidelines, being built as we go:

  • Come open minded. You might be an XXX developer and end up working on YYY technology. So what?
  • Come with an idea. Everyone should come with an OSS project that he wants to work on and share
  • Add your idea to our trello and put yourself as an assignee. if you don't have access to our trello yet, send a request to the organizer

How it works

  • We say hello and chat a bit waiting for everybody to arrive
  • We bring up the trello and everyone speak about the project they want to work on
  • We pair based on technology/project/affinity
  • We hack (possibly on several projects)
  • At the end, every group has 5 minutes to present their contribution
